Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Green Wolf Maintenance & Landscaping Inc. collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you visit greenwolf.work, request a quote, contact us, or use our client portal.

  • Effective date: April 20, 2026
  • Applies to website visitors, leads, and customers who contact Green Wolf through this site
  • Does not override the privacy practices of third-party websites or platforms we do not control

Quick summary

We collect the information needed to answer quote requests, schedule service, communicate with customers, and improve the site. We may use third-party services such as Google and Jobber to support those functions.

  • Name, phone, email, address, service details, and messages
  • Website usage and device data through analytics and similar tools
  • Client portal or embedded form activity handled by third-party providers

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly and limited technical information collected automatically when you use the site.

Contact and quote details

When you submit a quote or assessment request, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, property or service address, requested service, selected package, add-ons, and any notes or message you send.

Communication records

If you call, text, email, or message us, we may keep the communication and related details so we can respond, quote work, schedule service, or support an existing customer relationship.

Site and device data

We may collect information such as browser type, device type, pages viewed, approximate interaction data, referral source, and actions taken on the site through analytics, cookies, pixels, or similar technologies.

How We Use Information

We use personal information for practical business reasons, not because we need a giant legal document.

Provide quotes and service

We use your information to review requests, prepare estimates, communicate about jobs, schedule work, and manage customer service.

Operate the website

We use information to monitor form submissions, reduce spam, troubleshoot technical issues, and understand which pages or calls to action are working.

Business communication

We may contact you about the service you asked for, follow up on a request, send operational updates, or share relevant service information where permitted by law.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only when needed to run the site, manage requests, or comply with legal obligations.

Examples include service providers that help us process forms, host tools, manage customer requests, measure site performance, or operate widgets and client portal features. We may also disclose information if required to comply with law, enforce our terms, protect rights or safety, or support a business transfer.

If you click through to a client portal, embedded work request form, review widget, or another third-party destination, that provider may collect information directly under its own privacy practices.

Current third-party tools on this site

  • Google for analytics, advertising measurement, and form processing connected to a Google Apps Script endpoint
  • Jobber for the client portal and some customer request workflows

Third-party providers control their own systems. You should review their policies if you use those services directly.

Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking

This site uses cookies or similar technologies to understand site traffic, measure ad performance, and improve the website experience.

These tools may record information about pages visited, links clicked, referring pages, browser or device details, and similar interaction data. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if you block them.

What this means in plain English

If someone lands on the site, clicks a call button, opens the client portal, or submits a form, some of that activity may be measured so Green Wolf can see what is producing real leads and what is wasting money.

Retention and Security

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for quoting, customer service, business records, backup, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or legitimate business needs.

No website is perfectly secure. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Important limit

If you send information through third-party platforms, their security practices also matter. That part is not fully under Green Wolf control.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live and the laws that apply, you may have the right to request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, withdraw consent in some situations, or request deletion where legally allowed.

You may also opt out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the message or by contacting us directly. We may still contact you about active quotes, services, billing, or operational updates.

This website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the site.
