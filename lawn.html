Lawn add-ons explained

Tell us which extras you want in the quote form. Tap “Read more” to see what each add-on covers.

Core aeration

Popular add-on

Relieves compaction so water, air, and nutrients reach the roots.

Best done in spring or fall—core plugs naturally break down to feed the lawn.

Pairs well with overseeding for thicker turf and fewer bare spots.

Power rake / dethatch

Clean-up boost

Lifts matted debris so grass stands upright and new growth isn’t smothered.

Removes heavy thatch and winter debris before regular mowing starts.

We collect and haul away the debris so your yard is ready for fresh growth.

Overseeding & fertilizer

Growth add-on

Fills thin areas and feeds the lawn for colour and resilience.

Seed blend matched to Saskatoon lawns with a starter or weed-control fertilizer.

Ideal after aeration or dethatching when the soil is open and ready.
