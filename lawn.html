Mix-and-match lawn services

Add any combination of the options below. We’ll bundle them into one quote along with your mowing program.

Aeration

Pulls small plugs so water, air, and nutrients reach the roots for thicker, healthier turf.

Dethatching

Lifts and removes the matted thatch layer so new grass can breathe and fill in thin areas.

Fertilizer program

Season-long feeding plan tailored to Saskatoon weather for steady colour and growth.

Overseeding

Adds fresh seed to bare or tired spots after prep work so the lawn thickens evenly.

Top dressing

A thin layer of compost/soil that levels small dips and feeds the lawn between services.

Lawn rolling

Light roll to press down frost heaves and smooth bumpy spots without compacting the soil.

Garden bed edging

Hand-cut, crisp edges where lawn meets beds or sidewalks for a neat, defined border.

Tree / shrub shaping

Prunes small trees and shrubs to remove dead growth, lift canopies, and keep tidy silhouettes.
