One-time lawn add-ons

One-time treatments with long-term benefits

Aeration

Thicker turf and improved water absorption for stressed lawns.

Read more

Core aeration relieves soil compaction and allows air, water, and nutrients to reach the root zone.

  • Core aeration to improve root growth and soil health
  • Improves drainage and reduces runoff
  • Recommended in spring or early fall
  • Pairs well with: Overseeding, Fertilizer Program

Dethatching

Removes excess thatch so your lawn can breathe and grow.

Read more

Power raking removes built-up thatch that blocks water and nutrients from reaching the soil.

  • Removes dead grass and organic buildup
  • Encourages healthier, thicker growth
  • Best done in spring or early fall
  • Pairs well with: Aeration, Overseeding

Spot or full-lawn weed treatments designed to control common broadleaf weeds.

Overseeding

Fills in thin areas and improves lawn density.

Read more

Overseeding introduces fresh grass seed to improve thickness and overall turf appearance.

  • Helps repair bare or thin patches

  • Most effective when paired with aeration
  • Pairs well with: Aeration, Top Dressing

Top-Dressing

Levels low spots and improves soil quality.

A thin layer of quality soil or compost applied to enhance lawn structure and root health.

  • Supports strong root development
  • Pairs well with: Overseeding, Aeration
