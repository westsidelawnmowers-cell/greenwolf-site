Privacy Commitment

How we protect your information.

Green Wolf Maintenance & Landscaping Inc. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

Your data, handled with care

This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your information. Reach out anytime if you have questions.

Contact our team

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information directly from you when you request a quote, contact us, or use our services. This may include:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Street address
  • Email address
  • Service details you provide
  • Any additional information you choose to share

We may also collect basic technical information when you visit our website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide snow clearing, lawn care, and landscaping services
  • Contact you with quotes, updates, and service notifications
  • Respond to your questions or messages
  • Improve our services, marketing, and customer experience
  • Send invoices, receipts, and payment confirmations
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your information may be shared with:

  • Service providers helping with scheduling, invoicing, communications, and advertising
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) when you submit information through lead forms
  • Government or regulatory authorities if required by law

All third parties must protect your information.

4. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While no system is completely secure, we work to safeguard your data responsibly and professionally.

5. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access your information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to improve performance and user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.
