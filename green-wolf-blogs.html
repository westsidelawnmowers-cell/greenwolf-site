How often should I mow in Saskatoon?

Most homes do best with weekly mowing in peak season, then slower in cooler months to protect root strength and color.

Read

Mow weekly when growth is fast. During cooler weeks, move to every 10-14 days. Keep the blade sharp and avoid cutting too short, especially in hot weather.

What should I do before a spring cleanup?

Clear small items, mark delicate areas, and keep gates open so the cleanup team can work faster and safer.

Read

Move toys, hoses, and small tools before arrival. Mark fragile plants or sprinkler heads. If there are locked gates, share access details early to avoid delays.

What snow plan should I choose?

If you want predictable pricing and consistent visits, seasonal plans are usually the safest choice for busy households.

Read

Per-push is useful for low-snow periods, but monthly or seasonal plans reduce stress and price surprises. Seasonal service is best when reliability matters most.

Which landscaping upgrade gives fast results?

Bed edging, cleanup, and fresh mulch often give the fastest curb-appeal improvement without full redesign costs.

Read

Start with edge cleanup and mulch refresh for the biggest visual lift. Then add focused planting where people first see your property: front entry and driveway borders.
